How we created Seeker

The Seeker idea came from two sepreate visions that were merged into one.

We wanted to connect people of all ages and all backgrounds, everyone should be able to do something fun on their freetime.

Helping people connect while at the same time, having certain activies that will be benefical to the mind and body is a big deal.

Goals & Hopes

Connect people throughtout all 5 NYC boroughs.

Expand into other states & countires.

Build our own facilites to house our own events and activies.

Give back to all the vendors who used our site & help us grow.
